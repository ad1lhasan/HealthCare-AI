Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Healthcare AI. If you need more help, our support team is here for you.

What is Healthcare AI?
Healthcare AI is an AI-powered platform designed to help users manage their health, book appointments, set reminders, and get health information.
Is my data secure?
Yes, your data is encrypted and stored securely. We value your privacy and never share your information with third parties.
Can I get medical advice?
Healthcare AI provides information and support, but it does not replace professional medical advice. Always consult a healthcare provider for medical concerns.
How do I book an appointment?
Go to the Appointments section, fill in the required details, and submit your request. You will receive a confirmation and reminders.
How do I set a pill reminder?
Navigate to the Pill Reminder section, enter your medication details and time, and save the reminder. You will receive notifications as scheduled.
Who can I contact for support?
You can contact our support team via the Contact page or email us at support@healthcareai.com.

Need more help?

Our support team is available to assist you with any questions or issues you may have.
